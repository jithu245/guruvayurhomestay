Things to Do Near Guruvayur Homestay

Explore the Sacred & the Scenic

Sri Guruvayur Krishna Temple

One of the most sacred Hindu temples in India, dedicated to Lord Krishna. Known as the 'Dwarka of the South', this ancient temple attracts millions of pilgrims annually. The temple follows strict Kerala traditions and offers unique darshan experiences.

1.5 km  5 min auto

Mammiyur Mahadeva Temple

Ancient Shiva temple located near the main Guruvayur shrine. It is believed that a visit to Guruvayur Temple is incomplete without visiting Mammiyur Temple. Dedicated to Lord Shiva with beautiful Kerala architecture.

1.5 km  7 min

Punnathurkotta Elephant Sanctuary

Home to over 50 temple elephants, this unique sanctuary is a must-visit. Watch these majestic creatures up close in their natural habitat. One of the largest elephant sanctuaries in India.

3 km  12 min

Chavakkad Beach

A serene beach on the Arabian Sea coast, approximately 7 km from Guruvayur. Perfect for sunset views and peaceful walks. Less crowded than other Kerala beaches, making it ideal for families.

7 km  20 min

Guruvayur Temple Pond (Rudratheertham)

The sacred temple pond where devotees take a holy dip before temple darshan. Believed to have purifying properties. A significant part of the Guruvayur pilgrimage experience.

1.5 km  5 min

FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

How far is Guruvayur Homestay from the Temple?
Guruvayur Homestay is located approximately 1.5 km from the Sri Guruvayur Krishna Temple, which is about a 5-minute auto-rickshaw ride. We can also arrange temple visit transportation for our guests.
What are the room types and prices?
We offer two properties: (1) White Villa - Deluxe AC Room (₹2,500 + GST), Quadruple AC Room (₹4,000 + GST), or full villa at ₹14,000 + GST; (2) Heritage Pool Villa - Quadruple Bed Room (₹5,200 + GST), Double Deluxe Room (₹3,000 + GST), or full villa at ₹18,000 + GST. Contact us for availability and seasonal rates.
Do you provide food at the homestay?
Yes! We serve premium home-cooked Kerala meals (Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner). We are well-known for our traditional Kerala Sadhya, perfect for weddings, Choroonu, and family gatherings.
Is there a swimming pool?
Yes, we have a beautiful outdoor swimming pool available for guests booking rooms or the full Heritage Pool Villa. The pool area is private and set within our landscaped garden.
Can I book for a wedding or engagement?
Absolutely! We are a premier venue in Guruvayur for weddings, engagements, Haldi/Mehndi ceremonies, and Choroonu events. We offer full property rental, traditional catering (Sadhya), and complete event coordination.
How do I reach Guruvayur from Kochi Airport?
Cochin International Airport (COK) is approximately 79 km away (~2-hour taxi ride). We can arrange a comfortable airport pickup for you. You can also reach us via Guruvayur Railway Station (500m) or Thrissur Railway Station (30 km).
Is parking and WiFi available?
Yes, we provide free on-site parking and high-speed WiFi for all our guests at no additional charge.
What are the check-in and check-out times?
Standard check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 1:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out may be possible based on availability. Please contact us in advance to arrange.
